01 Alloq

Alloq is a robust SaaS solution, and the ultimate tool for managing allocations in institutional asset management, such as pension funds and insurers. Alloq provides portfolio managers with a secure and transparent environment in which they have full control over Alloq’s internal rulesets and calculations. Alloq can be adjusted to suit any strategy, now and in the future.

Visit website alloq.nl

  • Supports the whole spectrum

    From cash allocations and capital call management to [factor] rebalancing and simulations.

  • Client specific

    Adjust the ruleset to suit your client’s needs in a controlled environment.

  • Supports the whole process

    In full control of the process, including (market) data validation and four eyes principle.

  • Modules

    Alloq offers multiple modules that can be linked together, with the ability to improve frontend processes across multiple departments.

  • API integration

    Build on top of Alloq’s API to integrate your portfolio data into your own infrastructure.

  • Secure and certified

    Recognized by leaders within the industry, ISAE 3402 type II audited each year, and ISO 9001 & 27001 certified.

02 ProductTwo

ProductTwo is a lightweight digital automation tool that helps you generate complex documents for the financial market. It helps you to avoid mistakes, and spend less time on dull documents. Just fill in the forms, and we do the rest.

Visit website producttwo.com

  • Customizable

    Transforms your data into company branded documents.

  • Multilingual

    Easy set-up for multilingual supported documents. Generate documents in any language.

  • Safe and secure

    A protected environment, closed off to external parties.

03 Pension Funds Dashboard

The Pension Funds Dasboard offers board members and other colleagues direct and quick insights into how well their pension funds are performing.

Visit website producttwo.com

  • Gather insights

    A dashboard that offers insights into how well the pension funds are performing at a glance. More detailed insights are accessible from the overview.

  • KPI’s

    The most important KPI’s are positioned on one page, while more detailed information on those KPI’s are made available on click.

  • Customizable

    The Pension Funds Dashboard is fully customizable to your own KPI’s and corporate identity.
